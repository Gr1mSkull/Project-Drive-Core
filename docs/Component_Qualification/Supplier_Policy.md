# Supplier Policy

**Document ID:** CQP-SUP-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Proposed  
**Work Package:** WP-002

## 1. Purpose

Define rules for supplier and sourcing channel selection during qualification.

## 2. Scope

Procurement channels for prototype and production builds. This policy does **not** name or recommend specific distributors.

## 3. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Implementation Engineer | Record supplier evidence in qualification report when tasked |
| Program / Architect | Approve authorized supplier list (TBD) |

## 4. Inputs

- Authorized supplier list (TBD — OPEN ISSUE)
- Distributor stock and authenticity documentation

## 5. Outputs

- Supply chain risk section in qualification report
- No distributor recommendation in framework documents

## 6. Policy

1. Components shall be sourced from **authorized** manufacturer or franchise distribution channels for production.
2. Counterfeit risk shall be assessed for high-reliability and safety-related parts.
3. Grey market or unverified brokers require explicit risk acceptance.
4. Supplier identity shall be recorded in qualification report **References** when known.
5. This framework shall not freeze or recommend a distributor.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

| AC-1 | Policy avoids naming distributors |
| AC-2 | Counterfeit and authorization addressed |
